The annual Children's Day Hunt is being organised on 12th November, 2019 - Tuesday, from 9am to 11:59pm. Some general rules for the event:
  • Participants can be in a team of 1-3 students, all from the same batch.
  • No collaboration between teams.
  • A channel on Slack will be created, but any announcements made there will also be posted on the website. Therefore it is optional to join the Slack workspace.
  • Hints will be posted periodically. Asking for hints from organisers will get you banned.
  • Any offensive team names, or abuses on the slack workspace will get your team disqualified.
  • There will be 1 team selected from each batch and 3 wild-card entries for the On-Site round which will be held on 14th November, 2019.
  • You can contact us on our Email: minet@themis.in or DM us on Instagram.
